Globe Chess (Game Prototype)

 

In the year 2008, a notion came into my mind: In ancient times, people thought that the Earth was a plane, so they created flat-board games such as Chaturanga, the origin of all chess-like board games. But now we all know that the Earth is in fact an orb, so why don’t we make a modern version chess and play it on a globe?
