Mad Evolution (Interactive Installation)

 

The winner of ACM SIGGRAPH SpaceTime (USA)

in interactive-physical category on September 18th, 2009.

 

“Mad Evolution” is an interactive installation with physical components and digital contents work together seamlessly. You can manipulate the evolution process of life in any way you want, and witness how different the outcomes are with an unforgettably amusing experience.
