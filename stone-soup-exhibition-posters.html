Stone Soup Exhibition Posters (Printed Posters)

 

“Stone Soup” is the title of an interactive installation exhibition in the end of the sophomore year in my university, which is themed “house living” and lots of astonishing concepts and works are displayed in that exhibition, including the famous “Globe Chess” as a perfect decoration for living room as well as a suitable ornamentation for study room.

 

As the art director of this exhibition, I use the image of “impact crater” to give the posters (vertical banners) of this exhibition an unique style. By using the soft yet durable properties of paper, I was able to not only recreate the visual effect of a huge amount of meteor impacts but also arrange them wherever I want.
