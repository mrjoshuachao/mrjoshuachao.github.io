A Place That Air Flows (Solo Exhibition)

 

There are countless of so-called artists, using all kinds of pigment, paint, wood, metal and other materials—which is not created by themselves—to create pieces of artwork, and holding “solo exhibitions” all around the world.

 

If God, assuming God exists, knows these people, who cannot even create a single atom, are using all the already-existing materials to proudly create and exhibit their artworks like they actually create something, how will the God feel?

 

“A Place That Air Flows” is a piece of contemporary art, which is essentially a fake solo exhibition of God’s work. The interesting thing is, although the poster shows the specific time and place the exhibition takes, the exhibition is actually already exists in the very long period of time before, and will continuously exist after the time of exhibition. Furthermore, audiences will notice that  the “work” itself is not only exhibited in the place of the exhibition takes, but literally everywhere in the world.

 

This work reminds us that the most great and powerful work needs no exhibition nor worship from people. It is already there, and will continuously be there timelessly and placelessly.
