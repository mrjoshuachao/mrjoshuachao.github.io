Here And Now (Digital Poster)

 

This digital poster is meant to introduce the intriguing history of a small yet tall island on the surface of the planet Earth, which was claimed to be the colony by several authorities from different nations all around the world.

 

Although these authorities are eventually banished or retreated from the island hundreds of years ago and no longer pose any influence today, we as the current residents of this beautiful island are very welcome people to visit and take a vacation here again, to see how wonderful this place is now, and how it is worthy of its original Portuguese name “Formosa”.
