generated by the song's acoustic fingerprints

A great way to show off a song’s ownership when shared on the social media

 

A portal into the song’s STORY. Clicking into the DSD also allows viewers to catch a

glimpse of a song’s creative process and origination

COPYRIGHT PUBLIC OWNERSHIP

Taking your song “Public”

OurSong allows a project owner, under the consent of all

other copyright shareholders of the song, to initiate the

event of "Copyright Public Ownership (CPO)" so that fans

can buy into shares of the copyright and share future copy-

right royalties received as well as trade their copyright

share on the market.

．CPO allows many people co-own a piece of music copyright using the trust-free benefits

of blockchain technology

 

．Change the dynamics in with you engage with fans by allowing them to buy into a share

of a song, allowing them to share future revenue

 

．This “crowdfunding” like mechanism simultaneously provides musicians with necessary

marketing and production resources

REFERENCING / INHERITANCE

Finally, a way to

make sampling and remixing 100% legit

We use blockchain technology to track of contribution and

address issues like sampling, remixing, referencing and

other forms of derivative interpretations of creative works.

．Set a portion of your song's ownership to song(s) which you have either referenced or

sampled from or remixed

 

．A song can also have multiple references or sample sources where all original creators

will be honored and rewarded with the corresponding royalty splits

 

．OurSong allows multiple "generations" of sampling of a song in which the royalty splits

will cascade all the way through generations of "ancestors"
