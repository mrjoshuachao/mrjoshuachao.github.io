FEATURE #2

DIGITAL SONG DEED

The “Stock Certificate”

of your song

As all records are kept on Ethereum, we felt we

should visualize your ownership share so we

created a Digital Song Deed, or DSD, giving you

a digitally tangible and socially engaging way

to represent your ownership.

．

 

 

．

 

 

．

 

 

．

A dynamic, server-rendered virtual certificate with unique

motion graphics generated by the song's acoustic fingerprints

 

Displays cover artwork, creators information and its Ethereum

smart contract address

 

A great way to show off a song’s ownership when shared on

the social media

 

A portal into the song’s STORY. Clicking into the DSD also

allows viewers to catch a glimpse of a song’s creative process

and origination

FEATURE #3

REFERENCING / INHERITANCE

Finally, a way to make sampling

and remixing 100% legit

We use blockchain technology to track of

contribution and address issues like sampling,

remixing, referencing and other forms of

derivative interpretations of creative works.

．

 

 

．

 

 

 

．

Set a portion of your song's ownership to song(s) which you

have either referenced or sampled from or remixed

 

A song can also have multiple references or sample sources

where all original creators will be honored and rewarded

with the corresponding royalty splits

 

OurSong allows multiple "generations" of sampling of a song

in which the royalty splits will cascade all the way through

generations of "ancestors"

FEATURE #5

COPYRIGHT

PUBLIC OWNERSHIP

Taking your song “Public”

OurSong allows a project owner, under the

consent of all other copyright shareholders of

the song, to initiate the event of "Copyright

Public Ownership (CPO)" so that fans can buy

into shares of the copyright and share future

copyright royalties received as well as trade

their copyright share on the market.

．

 

 

．

 

 

 

．

CPO allows many people co-own a piece of music copyright

using the trust-free benefits of blockchain technology

 

Change the dynamics in with you engage with fans by

allowing them to buy into a share of a song, allowing them

to share future revenue

 

This “crowdfunding” like mechanism simultaneously provides

musicians with necessary marketing and production resources

FEATURE #6

OURCOIN

An unified simpler way of

transacting payments

OurCoin (OUR) is a virtual currency used by

the OurSong platform. It is based on Ethereum

according to the ERC20 Token Standard and its

total amount of issuance is capped at 300,000,000

．

 

 

 

．

 

 

．

OurCoin is a utility token that assists musicians and fans

from anywhere in the world on OurSong to earn, spend or

simply “HODL”

 

Earn a “Creator’s Gratuity” in OurCoin proportional to your

royalty payments for contributing to our new economy

 

In the future, we hope OurCoin will encourage users to offer

different professional services and sell merchandise on

OurSong
