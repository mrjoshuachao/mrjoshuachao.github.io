Just like a start-up company, gather your writing and production team together and get

started on producing your song, which is your “product”. OurSong provides each song-

in-progress with its own News Feed which we call STORY. Share your song’s journey of

creation, inspirations, ideas, milestones and accomplishments before its official release.

When a song’s complete, it’s time to register a song’s copyright information. Similar to a

stock certificate, a Digital Song Deed (DSD) is a visualized representation of your ownership

share on Ethereum, giving you a digitally tangible and socially engaging way to show your

ownership.

Musicians can initiate a Copyright Public Ownership (CPO) event so that fans can buy into

a portion of the song’s copyright and share future royalties.

Sampling, remixing and referencing elements of other songs is a popular practice in

modern music. Using blockchain technology, we can now seek permissions, record

adaptation information and compensative attribution, resulting in a trust-free yet more

efficient clearance and compensation process for derivative forms of creative adaptation.

Now that your song is complete and registered, it’s time to ship it to the global marketplace. OurSong provides creators direct access to all leading digital music streaming platforms.

Creators set agreed terms and we will ensure every contributor is fairly and promptly

compensated.
