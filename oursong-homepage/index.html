OurSong is the world’s first online music community-marketplace

Reimagining The Music Business

Copyright Public Ownership

One Song, a Million Shareholders

OurSong redesigns the commerce of music by inverting the value chain

through a new practice called Copyright Public Ownership (CPO). Through

the use of blockchain technology, we are redistributing value

and compensation in the music industry.

．A paradigm shift to the meaning of owning music.

．In the past, labels funded musicians to make music to sell to fans.

．From now onwards, songs can be owned by its creators and fans.

．This fuels a self marketing engine in the digital music economy.

Samples and remixes are very useful and important in modern music but

clearing copyrights can be time consuming and expensive. We use block-

chain technology to record of each party’s contribution and address fasting

clearance and appropriate compensation for sampling, remixing, referencing

and other derivative forms of creative works.

OurCoin (OUR) is a virtual currency used by the OurSong platform. It is based

on Ethereum according to the ERC20 Token Standard and its total amount of

issuance is capped at 300,000,000.

A Digital Song Deed (DSD) is a visualized representation of your ownership

share on Ethereum, giving you a digitally tangible and socially engaging way

to show your ownership.

OurSong provides creators direct access to the global music marketplace.

Creators set agreed terms and we will ensure every contributor is fairly and

promptly compensated.
