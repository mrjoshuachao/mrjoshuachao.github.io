Taking your song “Public”

Finally, a way to make sampling and remixing 100% legit

OurSong allows a project owner, under the consent of all other

copyright shareholders of the song, to initiate the event of

"Copyright Public Ownership (CPO)" so that fans can buy into

shares of the copyright and share future copyright royalties

received as well as trade their copyright share on the market.

We use blockchain technology to track of contribution and

address issues like sampling, remixing, referencing and

other forms of derivative interpretations of creative works.

．CPO allows many people co-own a piece of music copyright using the trust-free

benefits of blockchain technology

 

．Change the dynamics in with you engage with fans by allowing them to buy into

a share of a song, allowing them to share future revenue

 

．This “crowdfunding” like mechanism simultaneously provides musicians with

necessary marketing and production resources

．Set a portion of your song's ownership to song(s) which you have either

referenced or sampled from or remixed

 

．A song can also have multiple references or sample sources where all original

creators will be honored and rewarded with the corresponding royalty splits

 

．OurSong allows multiple "generations" of sampling of a song in which the

royalty splits will cascade all the way through generations of "ancestors"

An unified simpler way of transacting payments

OurCoin (OUR) is a virtual currency used by the OurSong platform.

It is based on Ethereum according to the ERC20 Token Standard and

its total amount of issuance is capped at 300,000,000.

OurCoin is a utility token that assists musicians and fans from anywhere in the world on

OurSong to earn, spend or simply “HODL”

 

Earn a “Creator’s Gratuity” in OurCoin proportional to your royalty payments for

contributing to our new economy

 

In the future, we hope OurCoin will encourage users to offer different professional services

and sell merchandise on OurSong
