One Song, a Million Shareholders

OurSong redesigns the commerce of music by inverting the value chain

through a new practice called Copyright Public Ownership (CPO). Through

the use of blockchain technology, we are redistributing value and compen-

Just like a start up company, gather your writing and production team toge-

ther and get started on producing your song. OurSong's provides each song-

in-progress its own News Feed which we call STORY. Share your song’s cre-

ation journey, inspirations, ideas, milestones and accomplishments with fans

before its official release.

When a song’s complete, it’s time to register a song’s copyright information.

Similar to a stock certificate, a Digital Song Deed (DSD) is a visualized rep-

resentation of your ownership share on Ethereum, giving you a digitally tan-

gible and socially engaging way to show your ownership.

Musicians can initiate a Copyright Public Ownership (CPO) event so that fa-

ns can buy into a portion of the song’s copyright and share future royalties.

Sampling, remixing and referencing elements of other songs is a popular

practice in modern music. Using blockchain technology, we can now seek

permissions and record contributions resulting in a faster and effective cle-

arance and compensation process for derivative forms of creative works.

Now that your song is complete and registered, it’s time to ship it to the glo-

bal marketplace. OurSong provides creators direct access to all leading digi-

tal music streaming platforms. Creators set agreed terms and we will ensu-

re every contributor is fairly and promptly compensated.

OurCoin (OUR) is a virtual currency issued by OurSong. It’s an essential and

integral part of OurSong designed to facilitate positive economic, psycholo-

gical and social impact for the long-term growth of our creative community.
