Dot (Digital Board Game)

 

The winner of Red Dot Award (Germany)

in entertainment category on October 23rd, 2010.

 

“Dot” is a digital board game which features a spherical, border-less board and science-fiction pieces such as Robot, Transporter and Satellite. Since the spherical board has no boundary and all pieces on it can move without limit of steps, players will find that the experience of playing “Dot” is totally different from any other board game.
