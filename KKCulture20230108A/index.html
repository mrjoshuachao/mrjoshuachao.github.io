ChynaHouse Digital

 

ChynaHouse Digital is a Taiwan based urban music boutique music label and management company that celebrates urban hip hop, soul and R&B. It is currently home to Julia Wu, Kimberley Chen, Jinbo, Jocelyn 9.4.0 and a stellar roster of rising next generation Mandopop artists while having also been responsible for the successful debuts of award winning singer/songwriter ?te and hip hop supergroup Ching G Squad.

We Rock Creative

 

We Rock is the music label founded by Taiwanese pop rock uber band 831. Aside from being the band’s musical workshop and management company, We Rock also produces live concerts and tours as well as creative music projects for brands, films and upcoming artists.

SKR Presents

 

Founded by award winning super producer Skot Suyama, SKR Presents is home to singer/songwriters Vickey Chen, Sophie Chen and 李杰明 W.M.L. Its unique brand of pop music is one of the most streamed online.

RYM Entertainment

 

Founded by urban music maestro Felipe Z, RYM Entertainment is home to alternative hip hop trio SmashRegz, rapper Troutfresh and has been an instrumental force in shaping the Taiwanese urban pop music landscape. Through its sister brand, AOA Entertainment Lab, RYM is also the hand behind Taiwanese girl band H.U.R.

Transparent Arts

 

Founded by Kirby Lee, Kevin Nishimura and James Roh, Transparent Arts is an American music label and management firm that is creating a global platform for the best of the American and Asian entertainment business. Its represents a roster of artists including The Rose, Far East Movement, DUMBFOUNDED, Year of the Ox, Yultron, Big Banana, SATICA and ØZI.

WRKSHP

KKFARM

 

The future of creativity is changing and as the world of content diversifies, more and more creators are creating engaging work from their bedrooms and workspaces. KKFARM sees this emerging class of creators and works provide them with more opportunities to reach audiences everywhere.

Soundscape

 

Soundscape is Taiwan’s biggest music distribution platform representing the biggest catalog of independent music after the three global major recording companies. In the past six years of service, it has helped thousands of artists in the region generate billions of streams through state of the art features for royalty management, online promotion and data intelligence.

BIOS Group

 

BIOS aims to establish a sustainable ecosystem for Taiwanese creators, developing a business model that combines "creative agency" with "commercial consulting" and has assisted many creators in creating powerful, interesting and culturally valuable business projects. It owns the media platform BIOS Monthly as well as design platform BIOS Create and provides professional services such as creator representation and creative consulting.

KKLIVE

 

KKLIVE is a one of a kind live event production, marketing and promotions company that bridges and extends offline experiences online and vice versa. From live concerts, exhibitions to music festivals, KKLIVE harnesses the power of emerging technology to make experiences accessible and memorable to all who seek it. Whatever the size, scale or occassion, KKLIVE is devoted to tailoring the most spectacular experiences that transcends venue.

KKTIX

 

KKTIX is Taiwan’s number one ticketing platform. Available on iOS, Android and web, KKTIX is the go to platform for event organizers having helped create access for thousands of events to millions of eager event-goers.

KKTV

 

KKTV is Taiwan’s leading multi-platform video-on demand service. Featuring an extensive catalog of content from major broadcasters from Korea, Japan, Taiwan and China, KKTV continues to deliver innovative formats of original content to over a million users each month.

KKLAB

 

KKLab is a venture studio formed by 30 data scientists & venture technologists. Using its proprietary technology, it forms joint-ventures with industry experts to demystify machine learning and build valuable business together.

OurSong

 

OurSong is a blockchain based music co-creation platform and NFT marketplace co-founded by Grammy winning singer/songwriter John Legend, together with a team of seasoned technologists and music industry veterans including Twitch co-founder Kevin Lin and iNDIEVOX founder Pochang Wu. OurSong is building an ecosystem that affords better opportunities through collaboration to emerging artists by leveraging powerful emerging technologies to protect creativity.
