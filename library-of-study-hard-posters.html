Library of Study Hard Posters (Printed Posters)

 

As a student of communications design, I have the responsibility to make a final report to people about the result of my four-year study in the university. Since all my design projects start with very serious topics and along with strong cultural research, I found myself cannot help to study very hard in a library. That indeed makes me a good designer.

 

After all these days studied in the library, I thought it’s time to create my own one. But just before I started, I wanted to capture the feeling of study hard in the library so I took these two photos. The first photo shows the English letters “JC”, which is the abbreviation of the library studier Joshua Chao. The second photo shows the Chinese character which represents the surname “Chao” of Joshua. Later, I decided to make these photos the attached posters of the portfolio set.

 

The interesting thing of the making of these photos is, during the time of photo-shooting, the old library is decommissioned and the new library is opened in order to replace the old one. Therefore these two photos are taken in different libraries, and coincidentally record this historical event.
