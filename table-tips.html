Table Tips (Utensil With Icon Design)

 

Table manners are always difficult for people who live in different countries to remember and perfectly perform, but they are extremely important to follow especially when dinning with extremely important people.

 

To solve this serious problem we might someday suddenly encounter, I hereby present to people all around the world this life saving collection of utensils, which seems perfectly ordinary but, as a matter of fact, has little hidden icons on it in order to secretly provide real-time tips of table manners to the person who is using them without been discovered by anyone else. With the help of “Table Tips”, no matter how close you look at the tips icon, it seems you are just looking at your food.
