Baa! (Tile-based Board Game)

 

This is an intriguing board game about “enclosure”. Players take turns to place their own fences onto the triangular or hexagonal tiles (depends on different versions), once the fences are connected and form an enclosed area, the player who contributes the highest amount of fences is the owner of it, and scores according to the number of sheep within it.
