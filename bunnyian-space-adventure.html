Bunnyian Space Adventure (Laser Cutting Product)

 

This series of photograph is originally for the promotional posters (print) and e-card advertising (screen-based) for my own creative product “Bunnyian Space Adventure”. The product is essentially the untitled pieces of object which you can put them in use anyway you want.

 

The product is made by Medium Density Fiberboard (wood) with laser cutting technology.

 

In order to input the appropriate data to the laser cutting machine, I use Adobe Illustrator and SketchUp to create looped vector lines, which will ultimately become the outline of objects, and then convert the file into AutoCAD format. The most exciting production process is when the fiberboard is cut by high energy laser and beautifully take shapes in the blinking of an eye.
