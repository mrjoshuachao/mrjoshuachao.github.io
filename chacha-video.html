ChaCha (Natural Detergent Powder Cubes)

 

The winner of “household goods and daily necessities” of

Good Design Award (Japan) on September 29th, 2015.

 

Camellia Oleifera seed powder was commonly utilized as natural and powerful detergent in old times, but it was almost forgotten after petrochemicals emerge. By compressing the powder into fixed cubes, it is easier for users to take the appropriate volume for each time usage, avoid the disturbance of accidentally spatter, and saves a lot of space of transportation and storage.
