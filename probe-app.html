P.R.O.B.E. (Puzzle Game on iOS)

 

Since a supercomputer defeated the human chess champion in 1997, there is always a big question: Will the computer rules mankind one day?

 

Unfortunately, we don’t have time to think about this question anymore, because we have a bigger trouble right now in the outer space. According to the computer logs of our lab in the outer space, a severe malfunction had occurred to the “Crystal Laboratory Overlord Wireless Nano-computer”, as known as the “C.L.O.W.N.”, and it has transformed the whole lab into a huge maze. We are recruiting people to pilot the probe and fly into the lab to help us retrieve the crystals, and shutdown the out-of-mind computer.

 

The game title “P.R.O.B.E.” refers to the unmanned space drone in the game, which players can control in order to fly it into the maze-like laboratory and retrieve all crystals. This is also a way to shutdown the C.L.O.W.N. because it uses crystals as its power source. The puzzle is constructed by several laboratory components, such as valve, clockwork, transporter and cloaking device. The variation of the maze is infinite, and this game absolutely have puzzle geeks’ attention around the world.

 

Key features: A total of 200 mind-bending puzzles composed of 10 sophisticated components. 20 collectable mosaic images. 5 different chicly designed models of probe. Exquisite science-fiction artworks. Impressive music and sound effect performance.
