Take The Stairs Please (Posters On Elevator Doors)

 

These tall posters are for the door of the elevator on the ground floor, which are meant to attract people’s attention and persuade them into take the stairs for once—instead of take the elevator as they usually do—because there is an funny mini-exhibition among the stairs area. The three photograph collections “Climbing”, “Falling” and “Shooted” are made for this exhibition, and the last one was eventually selected and displayed in the exhibition.

 

Due to the posters are posted on the doors of the elevator and that made the elevator seem fully occupied, although it was actually not, people were not happy at first because they thought the elevator is unavailable, but later when they found it was a poster they were thoroughly amused.

 

After the posters were removed due to the legal period of time is ended, they are ordered to be re-post by the authority regardless of the regulation​—simply because of the posters are brilliant and there was an important group of foreign visitors coming—and the university needed to make the best impression to them by showing this creative work.
