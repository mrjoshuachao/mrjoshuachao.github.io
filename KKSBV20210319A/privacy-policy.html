Overview

 

We are in the midst of the most exciting media revolution in history. Streaming technology is changing the way we consume content and experiences.

 

We are KKStream, Asia’s leading streaming technology provider and service consultant that has been helping clients build their own vertical media for almost a decade.

 

With offices based out of Tokyo, Taipei and Kaohsiung, we have been enabling media and entertainment leaders in the region to power platforms for millions of users through our Blendvision, our proprietary range of API, turnkey and customized large scale enterprise services.

 

KKStream is a subsidiary of KKBOX Group, Asia’s leading media technology company.

Business Area

 

 •  Cloud-based content delivery solutions.

 

 •  Planning, developing and operating cloud-based media streaming platforms.

 

 •  Customizable content and licensing management systems.

 

 •  Consulting services including research, analysis and strategic planning for media businesses.
