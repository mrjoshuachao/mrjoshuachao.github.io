CNEX

 

Giloo is a start-up company that has since become Taiwan's largest documentary streaming service for Chinese-speaking world. In 2017, they had a remarkable opportunity to host the first-ever online film festival exclusively featuring over 80 feature-length documentaries.

Value Delivered

 

Days before the festival, Giloo realized their player failed to meet the highest standards to thoroughly protect copyrighted content. With only days to spare before the festival, they approached KKStream.

 

As a result of BlendVision team's efforts, Giloo complied with studio-approved security standards and was able to participate in the world's first online film festival exclusively for documentaries, giving thousands of attendees the chance to stream Giloo's full collection of documentaries online.
