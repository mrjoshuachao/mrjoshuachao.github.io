Do’s and Don’ts

Keep clean within the safty frame

All other designs should not be placed within the safty frame.

Only “Home Screen” is not subject to this limit.

Use it only on the white background

Do not use it on any other colors or a moving background.

Only “Home Screen” is not subject to this limit.

All kinds of effect are forbidden

Do not apply shadow, glow, frame or any other effects to the app icon.

Preserve the original color

Do not change or decrease color by applying grayscale or indexed color mode.
