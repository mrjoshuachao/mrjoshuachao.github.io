Ticketing, Live Streaming, Everything: KKTIX Live

KKTIX Immersive Live Streaming is Breaking Records, Booking Legendary Artists & Going Viral, Daily.

Since then, KKTIX has continued 'blowing up,' from live streaming music events to millions of music fans, to expanding its reach with the launch of KKTIX Live. KKTIX Live is Taiwan's first-ever live performance platform to integrate both the ticketing and the online viewing experience.

 

KKTIX Live (formerly named KKTIX Virtual Venue) launched in 2020, soon becoming Taiwan's leading ticketing platform and taking the live-streaming world by storm. The COVID-19 pandemic accelerated the development and need for live streaming. As a result, event organization—for entertainment, music, even enterprising seminars and internal training—is no longer limited by time and space.

 

Unlike prior business models, KKTIX Live unifies services, letting event organizers make a smooth digital transition from live stage performances to streaming online performances, and charging audiences admission. KKTIX Live is revolutionizing the possibilities for online transmission—for musical performances, art exhibitions, cultural events and far more.

 

KKTIX Live is the pulse of the people.

 

 

 

KKTIX Live: Payment Gateway, Copyright Protection, Identity Authentication and other Leading Technologies Featured

 

KKTIX Live's One-Stop Service Helps Event Organizers Transform

 

Taking a one-stop, unified approach sets KKTIX Live apart in the typical world of ticketing, or live streaming platforms. Instead, KKTIX Live integrates crucial services involved with holding live events—such as payment gateway, copyright management, identity authentication and information security. With one simple click, users can purchase tickets and watch live streaming events.

 

In addition to event essentials such as a secured payment gateway, KKTIX Live supports the industry-leading Digital Rights Management (DRM) system. This allows each ticket sold to be available for viewing in only one browser tab. KKTIX Live also uses a technology that limits the number of devices accessed, while assuring that link sharing, video recording and screenshots are prohibited to protect the copyright of content creators or event organizers.

 

KKTIX Live also uses Geo IP to prevent specific regions from accessing the streaming events, so organizers can make flexible adjustments to where that streaming is available based on the conditions of copyright licensing.

 

Event organizers now no longer need to be concerned about information security when coordinating their services between multiple platforms. KKTIX Live's one-stop service is a game changer for the events industry.

 

 

 

KKTIX Live's Upgraded Services: Initiate Live-Streaming Events With a Simple Click

 

Optimizing Effectiveness, Flexibility, Cost & the Communications Process

 

Whether it's for music concerts, talk shows, internal training or webinars, KKTIX Live platform helps you streamline online events, expanding your audience reach. KKTIX Live is dedicated to making live streaming user-friendly. We've optimized the management features for event organizers and business clients of all types and sizes. By simplifying the process of live streaming, KKTIX Live lets you conduct broadcasts globally, easily and independently.

 

The all-new KKTIX Live features are changing the way event organizers think. Blue Fantasy, a complex restaurant in Kaohsiung, birthplace of many popular artists, offers their audiences live performances. Then Blue Fantasy began offering their customary performances live streamed on KKTIX Live, so audiences who couldn't be there in person could still enjoy the shows.

 

"The uncertainty brought by the pandemic has made the 32-year-old Blue Fantasy realize there is a gap between us and the younger generations, which motivates us to seek change and innovation. Collaborating with KKTIX Live allows us to bring more fans to the performance venue, attract new audiences, and enable local bands to be discovered by more people," said Christine, Marketing Representative for Blue Fantasy.

 

Riverside, a famous  livehouse in Taipei, now uses KKTIX to build their business advantage. "KKTIX Live makes it possible for organizers to execute live streaming on their own. This not only helps cut down the cost of personnel and time, but also makes the planning of events more flexible. The KKTIX Live team responds to our technical questions promptly, and they're attentive whenever we are launching or in the middle of a live streaming event," said Miss Jhan, Marketing Representative from Riverside. "The intuitive, convenient process allows our staff to run the platform smoothly, ensuring successful events."
