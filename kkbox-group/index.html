KKBOX Group is a leading digital music technology firm in Asia. Started by a group of music loving Internet

software developers, we built and launched one of the world’s first music streaming services in 2005. Based

in Taipei, the heart of Chinese pop music, we gradually grew our business from Taiwan out to Hong Kong,

Singapore, Malaysia and Japan. Ever curious towards reinvention and discovering new business models of

the future, we have expanded our business scope from music streaming to live events, technology services,

content, investments and continue to explore reinvention through innovation in the digital entertainment space.

Taking The Online Experience Offline

Technology has disrupted the way content is distributed and consumed. KKFARM was inspired and established after identifying a clear new generation of content creators who deploy different technique to making content, making use of Internet tools that have appeared in the last decade. Likening then to Internet start-up entrepreneurs, we set up KKFARM to discover, invest and empower this new generation of creators.
