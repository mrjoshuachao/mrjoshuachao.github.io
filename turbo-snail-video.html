Turbo Snail (Action Game on iOS and Android)

 

This is a casual game with simple background story and beautiful paper-cut style of art. It might be the first digital game which uses pure hand-made paper art to build everything—from the stood still stage to the adorable animation of characters—in this game. The control of this game is gyroscope-based, which means it is highly intuitive for player so they don’t even have to read a single word of instruction to understand how to play. Therefore, it is an attractive game to pre-school children.
