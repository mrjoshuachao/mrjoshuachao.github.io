Dot for iOS (Board Game on iOS)

 

This project is meant to design an engaging user interface and experience for the iOS version of “Dot”. In order to keep player’s attention in the game itself instead of the interface, the design follows the principle of minimalism, which, in other words, is the design rule of “If you remember the shape of your spoon at lunch, it has to be the wrong shape.”
