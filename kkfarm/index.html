About

KKFARM is an evergreen investment firm creating new models to foster and fund a new generation of music brands. Inspired by the changing soundscape of Internet culture, KKFARM was formed in 2016 to discover, invest and empower the next wave of music artists, businesses and cultural brands through a hybrid mix of venture capital funding and technology aided strategy building rooted in data intelligence and analytical market insights.

Philosophy

KKFARM’s investment philosophy is rooted in our wish to fuel an ecosystem of new generation music businesses. We seek long term partnerships not based on exit multiples but rather sharing the struggle of building and growing with our portfolio companies. We see the benefits of the long game and believe in using investment opportunities to learn as we build together with our investees. The fundamental values behind our investment philosophy are:

New Dimensions

 

We seek music visionaries who carve out new cultural dimensions music business, adding to the cultural disruption of music happening right now.

Commitment

 

We do not seek short-term exits but rather look towards finding deeply committed teams with their own vision of building unique music brands of the future that contribute to the formation of a symbiotic music ecosystem.

Fellowship

 

We understand there is a tremendous amount of talent out in the music business. However, we are looking for a special breed of talented people who are immensely hungry yet brutally honest, humbly curious but shamelessly daring and passionately thoughtful while bravely determined. These are the qualities we seek in partnerships and are not afraid to face the challenges of building together with those are willing to embrace the change and innovation the future promises.

Investment

 

We focus our investments on the growth of “atomic” labels. Atomic labels are defined as highly functional lean-structured music brands that are able to groom artists, provide in-house production and oversee artist management under one roof. Atomic labels usually included one or more music producer, artist and manager. We identified four stages of growth for investments:

 

a. Pre-Labels (High-calibre teams with potential to become atomic labels)

b. Atomic label (w/ New Artist)

c. Atomic label (w/ Trending Artist)

d. Atomic label (w/ Established Artist)

Global Distribution

 

KKFARM’s digital music distribution platform, Soundscape, services some of the biggest names in Mandarin music and delivers to all of the world’s major music streaming platforms.

Beyond Investing

 

Aside from capital investments, KKFARM provides business development and back-office services to help our investees grow within an ecosystem of shared resources and opportunities. These services are tailored to the needs of each company and we continue to expand our offerings to create a robust media and technological network that complements our investment portfolio.

Reactive Analytical Data A&Ring (R.A.D.A.R) Methodology

 

As a technology driven company, KKFARM is committed to the development of data-driven business intelligence tools for creative strategy and development. As data analytics and artificial intelligence claim a more critical role in the future of the music business, we are committed to the development of new intuitive data technology that will not only provide musicians with better inspiration and creativity but also allow music businesses to function more efficiently with a better positional advantage. Together with KKBOX, we are developing a proprietary workflow known as R.A.D.A.R Methodology that we hope will redefine A&R in the Digital Age.
