Update

Posted by Julia Wu on September 20th (5 days ago)

It gave me a new sense of inspiration. I didn’t want to disrupt the integrity of her demo because it was a beautiful song. I didn’t feel like there was a need to over produce. Most producers wanna show off but I feel a real producer should bring out the soul of the song and not plaster his/her own “signature” on it.
