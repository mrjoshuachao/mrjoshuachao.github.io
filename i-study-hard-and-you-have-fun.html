I Study Hard And You Have Fun (Printed Portfolio)

 

As a student of communications design, I have the responsibility to make a final report to people about the result of my four-year study in the university. Since all my design projects start with very serious topics and along with strong cultural research, I found myself cannot help to study very hard in a library. That indeed makes me a good designer.

 

The title of this portfolio “I Study Hard And You Have Fun” refers to the attitude of a multimedia designer should always have, which is to work very hard for months or years and make people happy for seconds or minutes.

 

Long before this portfolio is made, I knew one day I will make one and it is not easy to collect everything for a four-year study in multimedia design in short time, therefore I started to collect everything in the beginning of the first year in the university. This data-collecting behavior affects me severely, and I am still gaining a lot of good outcomes from it even today.
