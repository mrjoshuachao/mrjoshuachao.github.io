Overview

 

KKStream is a video streaming service consultant and technology company that combines cutting-edge technology with robust knowledge of the

industry to deliver comprehensive video streaming solutions. With experience successfully kickstarting new streaming businesses and migrating multi-million user services to our scalable video streaming platform, we offer

the perfect balance of business and technology insight to help our clients find the video streaming solution exactly suited to their business needs and ensure they are up-to-date on the latest technology and market trends.
