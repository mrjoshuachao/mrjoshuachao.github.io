We are in the midst of the most exciting media revolution in history. Streaming technology is changing the way we consume content and experiences online. KKStream is Asia’s leading media technology provider and service consultant that has been helping clients build their own vertical media business for almost a decade. With offices in Tokyo, Taipei and Kaohsiung, we have enabled media and entertainment leaders in the region to power platforms for millions of users through BlendVision solutions, our proprietary range of API, turnkey and customized large scale enterprise services.

In the early Aughts, a group of music loving engineers who had been crazy about connecting people through Internet technology chanced upon the opportunity to solve a problem plaguing the music industry - digital piracy. They asked, “How might we provide music fans access to all the world’s music while bringing income back to the music industry?”. This began the quest to license millions of songs and create an experience that has since become the business of music streaming. Since then,  KKBOX has grown to become one of Asia’s most influential social audio brands, reshaping the Mandopop music landscape as well as the way we experience audio and connect with like-minded people, building a flourishing community between artists and fans.

KKTV is a video and knowledge technology service provider that builds and operates online video entertainment and knowledge platforms using state of the art internet streaming technology.

KKNOW is a live streaming social network company that reimagines how online live entertainment is experienced by creating innovative and interactive ways for artists, performers and fans to connect, compete and convene on the Internet, uncovering revenue streams for talent to thrive in digital economies.

KKLIVE is a one of a kind live event production, marketing and promotions company that bridges and extends offline experiences online and vice versa. From live concerts, exhibitions to music festivals, KKLIVE harnesses the power of emerging technology to make experiences accessible and memorable to all who seek it. Whatever the size, scale or occassion, KKLIVE is devoted to tailoring the most spectacular experiences that transcends venue. It operates Taiwan’s online ticketing platform KKTIX, live venues in both Taipei and Kaohsiung as well as a new “Virtual Venue” accessible anywhere on the Internet.

As the world of content becomes increasingly decentralized with thousands of independent musicians and labels creating content from their bedrooms or workspaces, KKFARM sees an emerging class of creators of the future that deserves direct access to their audiences. Be it digital distribution, productivity solutions or new channels increase exposure, KKFARM empowers creators to capture value while remaining in the driver’s seat of their creative freedom. Through a unique mix of technology and venture funding, KKFARM represents the biggest catalogue of music outside of the three international majors.

KKBOX is one of Asia’s leading audio streaming services in Taiwan, Hong Kong, Singapore, Malaysia and Japan. Since 2005, becoming the first legal music subscription streaming service in the world, KKBOX is constantly reimagining and redefining the way we experience music, podcasts and all forms of audio content by marrying technology and culture. With 11 million registered users, KKBOX continues to lead the charge in connecting people through their passions and remains one of the most influential media voices in the Chinese speaking world.

KKTV is Taiwan’s leading multi-platform video-on demand and live broadcasting service. Featuring an extensive catalogue of content from major broadcasters from Korea, Japan, Taiwan and China, KKTV’s continues to deliver innovative formats of original content to a fast growing subscription user base.

The KKBOX Music Charts and Awards (KMA) is Greater China’s most celebrated music charts and award show. Since 2006, our charts have remained the quintessential benchmark for fans, artists and labels in the region, highlighting the most beloved Western, Korean and Mandarin artists and songs. Our annual award show is broadcasted to millions of households throughout Asia bringing together the most memorable performances and collaborations by Asian and International artists.

KK書 (pronounced K-K-Shu) is Taiwan’s newest and most innovative subscription based audio knowledge platform that offers book summaries, audio books and exclusive podcasts for entertainment and education.

KKNOW is Taiwan’s first interactive live streaming talent competition app. Its unique Turntable feature allows contestants, judges and viewers to interact with each other in real time while taking part in different types of competitions from singing contests to freestyle rap, DJ and dance battles, allowing fans to show support through gifting and purchasing official digital merchandise.

Soundscape is Taiwan’s leading music distribution platform. Serving a rising segment of progressive independent music artists and labels, Soundscape allows music to be delivered worldwide to all major and minor streaming platforms with the click of a button.

Soundsgreat is a music label cloud engine that powers music labels and aggregators, allowing them to build their own online vertical music brands and services without having to build costly infrastructure and letting them focus on what matters most - empowering more creativity.
