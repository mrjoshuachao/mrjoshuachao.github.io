Iconbelieveit (YouTube Channel)

 

​Every icon is a precious. When you see one, instantly you get the idea. Now imagine this: A group of people, they love icon so much and they use only icons to communicate. They write, read, type and speak in icons, and they do have the icon dictionary. You know what? It’s real, and it’s happening right now! Ladies and gentlemen, you can believe in ​“Iconbelieveit”.

 

In every episode of this program, some interesting facts of a modern icon in our daily life will be introduced, and then, the Confucius will convert that modern icon into the related ancient icon, in order to show the unique artistic style, the funny icon-forming ideas and the 4,000-year-old icon families to the Youtube audiences worldwide.

 

This educational—and a little bit of entertaining—YouTube program is created, scripted, narrated and video-audio edited by Joshua Chao singlehandedly. This program is currently running without a specific time table, so the new episodes will be aired without a fixed schedule.
