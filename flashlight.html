Flashlight (Camera-based Game on iOS)

 

Flashlight is the prototype of the iOS puzzle game “P.R.O.B.E.” which is originally a camera-based game controlled by player’s gestures in front of the camera. When players make certain gestures in front of the camera to control the “flashlight”—a spot of illumination in the deep dark alien world of the game—to lead the main character all the way through the mysterious alien maze-like starship, and make path by driving away the jellyfish guards. The art style of the user interface is called “Space Jelly”, which perfectly fit the theme of this game.

 

There are five sections in the menu: Star chart, control switch, power management, achievement and worldwide high score. The star chart is a map of this game where players can explore it and select a level to enter. Control switch provides two ways for players to control the flashlight: Teleportation or Touch. Power management shows the quantity of the power of the main character's four key abilities, and allows players to add more power if they need. Achievement shows all the medals the player had earned. Last but not least, worldwide high score shows all players around the world and arrange them by score, so you can see if your score defeats your friends.
