Turbo Snail (Action Game on iOS and Android)

 

This is a casual game with simple background story and beautiful paper-cut style of art. It might be the first digital game which uses pure hand-made paper art to build everything—from the stood still stage to the adorable animation of characters—in this game. The control of this game is gyroscope-based, which means it is highly intuitive for player so they don’t even have to read a single word of instruction to understand how to play. Therefore, it is an attractive game to pre-school children.

 

There are three themed worlds in this game, which are grassland, bookstore and candy ground, and a total 30 levels in this game for the good-hearted snail to adventure and to help ants with their candy-collecting job. Once the snail starts to roll, it becomes a cart for ants to hitchhike, and that will not only help ants to get home faster in limited time, but also help them to avoid the dangerous spiders or the gaps on the ground.

 

Turbo Snail helps ants get candies home! There are 50 different snail shells available to switch! Enjoy the cute art-style and the upgraded shells of Turbo Snail! Key features: 50 shells with different powers. 3 themed worlds. Exquisite paper-cut artworks. Impressive audio performance.
